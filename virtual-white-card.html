Versatile Business Use Cases

Discover how Wasabi Virtual White Cards can streamline various aspects of your business operations and financial management.

Procurement

Create dedicated virtual cards for purchasing departments with predefined spending limits and vendor restrictions, ensuring budget compliance while maintaining flexibility.

Marketing Expenses

Issue specific cards for online advertising platforms, subscriptions, and campaign spending, with real-time tracking to optimize your marketing budget.

Travel & Expenses

Simplify employee travel expenses by issuing temporary cards for specific trips with appropriate limits, reducing reimbursement complexity and improving spend visibility.

Software Subscriptions

Manage your SaaS subscriptions with dedicated cards for each service, preventing unexpected charges and making it easy to track recurring expenses.

Departments

Allocate specific budgets to different departments with dedicated virtual cards, providing autonomy while maintaining central oversight of all transactions.

Project Management

Create cards for specific projects to track expenses against budgets in real-time, simplifying accounting and improving project financial management.

Advanced Control Features

Spending Limits

Set daily, weekly, or monthly spending limits for each virtual card to ensure budget compliance and prevent overspending.

Merchant Restrictions

Limit card usage to specific merchant categories or individual vendors to control where funds can be spent.

Time-Based Controls

Set card expiration dates or activate cards only during specific time periods to enhance security and control.

Real-Time Notifications

Receive instant alerts for all transactions, declined purchases, or when spending approaches predefined limits.

Instant Freeze/Unfreeze

Pause and resume any virtual card with a single click to address security concerns or manage spending.

Role-Based Access

Assign different permission levels to team members, controlling who can create, modify, or view specific virtual cards.
