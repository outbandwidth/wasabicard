Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a Wasabicard?

Applying for a Wasabicard is simple! Download our app, complete the registration process with your personal information, verify your identity, and your virtual card will be ready immediately. Physical cards are typically delivered within 3-5 business days.

What are the fees associated with Wasabicard?

Wasabicard offers a transparent fee structure with no hidden charges. Basic accounts have no monthly fees, while premium accounts have a small monthly fee that's waived when you meet certain spending thresholds. Currency exchange is available at competitive rates, and international transactions have minimal fees.

How does the cashback program work?

Our cashback program rewards you for every purchase you make with your Wasabicard. Standard accounts earn 1% on all eligible purchases, while premium accounts can earn up to 2% on select categories. Cashback is automatically credited to your account each month and can be used immediately.

Can I use Wasabicard internationally?

Yes! Wasabicard is designed for global use. You can make purchases in multiple currencies with competitive exchange rates and minimal foreign transaction fees. Our virtual and physical cards are accepted anywhere Visa is accepted worldwide.

How secure is Wasabicard?

Security is our top priority. Wasabicard uses bank-level encryption to protect your data and transactions. You can instantly freeze/unfreeze your card through the app, set spending limits, receive real-time transaction alerts, and our 24/7 fraud monitoring system provides an additional layer of protection.

What's the difference between virtual and physical cards?

Virtual cards are instantly available in your app and are ideal for online purchases and subscriptions. They offer additional security as you can create multiple cards for different purposes. Physical cards work just like traditional bank cards for in-person payments and ATM withdrawals. Both types offer the same core benefits and security features.
