Frequently Asked Questions

What are the qualification requirements?
For a referral to qualify, your friend must sign up using your referral code, complete the account verification process, and make at least one transaction of $20 or more within 30 days of account opening.
When will I receive my referral reward?
Both you and your friend will receive the $25 reward within 3 business days after all qualification requirements have been met.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?
No, there is no limit to the number of friends you can refer. However, there may be a maximum reward cap of $500 per calendar year for regulatory purposes.
Can I refer family members who live at the same address?
Yes, you can refer family members even if they live at the same address, as long as they are new customers who have not previously had a Wasabi Card account.
