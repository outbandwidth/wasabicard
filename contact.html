Frequently Asked Questions

What are your customer support hours?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or issues you may have. You can reach us by phone, email, or through our in-app chat feature.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, we recommend contacting our customer support team directly by phone for immediate assistance.

I'm interested in partnering with Wasabi Card. Who should I contact?

For partnership inquiries, please email our business development team at partnerships@wasabicard.com with details about your company and proposed collaboration.

How can I report a technical issue with my account?

To report technical issues, please contact our technical support team at support@wasabicard.com or through our in-app help center. Include details such as the device you're using and steps to reproduce the issue.
